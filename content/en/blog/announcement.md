# openDesk Edu Project Announcement

We are excited to announce the launch of the openDesk Edu project! This initiative brings the openDesk digital workplace to higher education institutions, connecting learning management, collaboration, and productivity under one open-source platform.

## What is openDesk Edu?

openDesk Edu integrates 15 open-source services into a unified platform designed specifically for universities and research institutions. From learning management systems like Moodle and ILIAS to collaboration tools like BigBlueButton and Nextcloud, everything works together seamlessly.

## Get Involved

We welcome contributions from the community — whether you're a developer, designer, educator, or institution looking to adopt open-source digital infrastructure. Join our community and help shape the future of educational technology.
